Hi, I'm Ezekiel!

I’m a global public‑health professional, researcher and innovation leader with 10 years of experience translating research and innovation to deliver better health outcomes across Africa, Europe and Latin America. About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an 8‑week live online interactive course that teaches you to automate data analysis, content creation, literature reviews and routine tasks—entirely without code.

  • Documents, Presentations & Websites – content generation & no‑code sites
  • Automated Data Analysis – Julius, ChatGPT Code Interpreter, Windsurf
  • GenAI for Research & Text Analysis – Elicit, LiteRev, summarisation, classification
  • Custom GPTs & Chatbots – RAG systems, Botpress, API integrations
  • AI Workflows for Task Automation – Lindy automations, Make.com, Gmail API
  • GenAI for Communication & Outreach – Ideogram, InVideo AI, marketing flows
  • Capstone Project & Showcase – design and present a real‑world AI solution
